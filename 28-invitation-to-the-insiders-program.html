We want you to get hands-on with all aspects of the platform. Try everything from creating an account, posting events, favoriting candidates, messaging potential hires, and even applying for jobs. When you come across something interesting (or confusing), we'd love to hear about it. Simply email care@edupassport.io and pen down the issue. For example: "Windows - Chrome - Issue with job application process."

We're not holding you back – feel free to explore the app to your heart's content. Push buttons, click links, and make it dance! Your feedback is invaluable in helping us make this platform as sleek and functional as possible.
